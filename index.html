FAQ

Common Questions

Are the pandits real and verified? +
Yes. Every pandit on our platform is personally interviewed, credential-verified, and trained in authentic Vedic traditions. Many belong to Kashi's centuries-old pandit families and hold degrees from Sampurnanand Sanskrit University.
Can I watch the puja live from abroad? +
Absolutely. We stream every puja live via WhatsApp video or Zoom. You'll receive a join link before the scheduled time. The full recording is also shared with you afterwards.
What if I don't know my Gotra? +
No problem. If your Gotra is unknown, we use the standard "Kashyap Gotra" which is traditionally used in such cases. Our pandits are experienced in handling this situation correctly.
How do I receive prasad if I am outside India? +
For NRIs, we send a digital prasad certificate, puja photos, and full HD video. For India-based devotees, physical prasad (Gangajal, rudraksha, sindoor, and sweets) is courier-dispatched within 48 hours of the puja.
What is your refund policy? +
If the puja is not conducted due to any reason on our end, you receive a full refund within 3 business days. Rescheduling is free up to 24 hours before the booked time. We stand by our 100% service guarantee.
